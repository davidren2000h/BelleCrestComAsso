2026 Neighborhood Association Annual Dues

You Can Now Pay via Zelle!

Thank you for your continued support!

Please complete the Bellecrest Neighborhood Association Dues Sheet to make sure we have your current contact information, using the handy envelope provided. Or you can now pay via our website. Your $25.00 yearly membership dues are used solely for Association purposes such as newsletters, copies, meeting room rentals, paint for our mailbox stands, landscaping, etc. The payment of dues also allows you to vote for board members and to vote on issues at the annual neighborhood association meetings.

Please make your checks payable to Bellecrest Neighborhood Association and enclose them in the same envelope as your dues sheet. Please mail to:

Erin Powell-Dilloo
Bellecrest Treasurer
1015 106th Ave. S.E.
Bellevue WA  98004

To pay via Zelle:
Please email yearlydues@bellecrest98004.org and make sure you give us your name, phone, address, and your priorities for the board.

Your dues have helped pay for our neighborhood entry signs, block parties, yard-waste dumpster day, landscaping, bus benches, and other improvements. Let us know your suggestions!

If you are a tenant, please pass this important newsletter and dues request to your landlord. We would greatly appreciate their contact information so that all owners can be notified, too. Please send your landlord's contact info to bellecrest.assoc@gmail.com.
